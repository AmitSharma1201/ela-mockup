Esports got its start way back during the arcade days. The earliest known video game competition ever was The "Intergalactic Spacewar Olympics", which happened in 1972 at Stanford University. Students gathered to compete in a video game called Spacewar! with the grand prize of a year's subscription to Rolling Stone magazine on the line. A year's subscription to Rolling Stone magazine was the grand prize? Sure, it wasn't the million-dollar prizes we see today, but this event marked the very beginning of competitive gaming.
